Frequently Asked Questions

← Back to Dashboard
How do I submit a new helpdesk ticket?
Go to your dashboard and click "Submit Ticket". Fill in the subject and description, then click submit.
How can I check the status of my complaint?
In "My Tickets", you’ll see a list of your tickets with their current status shown next to each.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Contact the college IT helpdesk to reset your password securely.
Can I delete a submitted ticket?
Currently, you can’t delete tickets for record keeping. You can mark it as resolved or submit a new ticket if needed.
What types of issues should I report?
Report technical issues (e.g., login problems), facility issues (e.g., projector not working), or any administrative queries.
